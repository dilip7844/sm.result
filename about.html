About App

'Quick Save' allows to download/Save whatsapp statuses and instagram posts. This app contained two modules as belows.

  • WhatsApp Status Saver

    • You can see your loaded, seen whatsapp statuses here. you can also Save, delete and reshare them. Before using this module, make sure you have official whatsapp installed in your device. Though you can change the location for whatsapp from setting if you have any mod of it.

  • Instagram Post Downloader

    • This module requires a instagram post link to show or download the post. It shows you the link content before downloading and provides you the option to download. You can directly share the post to this app or paste the instagram post link from clipboard ,it will load it itself.
